Objectives

To make a functional system for the data management of the enterprise to improve the client's satisfaction levels and increase the employee's production, making that the system can solve the problems suffered by the enterprises that doesn't count with a good organization.

To demonstrate a better efficiency and performance by the employee when the employee needs to realize his job so he can work harmoniously with the suministrator, giving to the clients a better satisfaction.

To classify the system in an ordered way to help to the data management of the enterprise with specialized production lines.

To create a trouble-free program so the users can use it easily.

Program functions

The "program functions" refers to a process control system as a way of keeping organized all the information obtained from the realization of mostly handmade products, where a series of tools are used to analyze the processes of a business or company, this in order to to maintain order within the employees and the company, since you can have a better organization through a system that keeps track of the processes done and to be done.
